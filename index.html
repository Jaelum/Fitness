Your Coach

Training that bridges the gap between the gym and the clinic

With a background in certified personal training and a specialization in corrective exercise, the EM Systems approach is built on one principle: strength fixes almost everything. Whether you're working around a past injury, dealing with chronic pain from muscular imbalances, or simply want to move and feel better — every program starts with understanding how your body actually moves, not how it's supposed to.

Based in San Francisco and working with clients online worldwide, EM Systems combines the precision of corrective exercise with the proven results of progressive resistance training. No fluff, no cookie-cutter plans — just a program built specifically for you.
